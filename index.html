How to Use This App

Getting Started

  • Navigate using tabs at the top
  • Choose Practice or Test mode
  • Select difficulty level for personalized learning
  • Click topics to start practicing

Practice vs Test Mode

  • Practice Mode: Unlimited hints, immediate feedback, learn at your pace
  • Test Mode: Timed questions, no hints, exam simulation

Mastery System

  • Achieve 80% mastery in each topic to complete it
  • Get detailed feedback and retry incorrect answers
  • Build your streak by practicing daily
  • Track detailed analytics of your performance

Answer Assessment

  • Answers evaluated on KEY CONCEPTS, not exact phrasing
  • Synonyms and paraphrasing are accepted
  • Maintain correct spelling and grammar
  • After 2 failed attempts, receive worked examples

Accessibility Features

  • üîä Text-to-speech: Click "Read Aloud" to hear questions
  • Adjustable difficulty levels
  • Keyboard navigation support

Gamification

  • Earn XP for correct answers (20 XP short, 50 XP long)
  • Level up every 100 XP
  • Unlock badges by completing challenges
  • Maintain daily streak for bonus rewards

Data Management

  • Progress saves automatically
  • Export/Import to backup or transfer devices
  • Download detailed reports for teachers
  • View comprehensive analytics
